## 🌙 Nightlife & Drinks

**SATURDAY NIGHT ROUTE — The Big Night Out**

| Venue | Type | Area | What to Try | Est. Cost |

| Ura Namba izakayas | Hidden dining district | East of Namba Station | Wander the narrow alleys, pick a busy spot | ¥2,000–3,000 |

**Suggested route**: Start at Ura Namba (20:00) → Metro to Tenma for standing bar crawl (21:00–22:00) → Metro back to Namba → Bar Freedom for whisky (22:30) → Bible Club for nightcap (23:30)

_Saturday is the biggest nightlife night — Osaka will be buzzing. Last trains ~midnight, or walk back to Namba from most spots._

## 📝 Notes

> 📌 **Book ahead**: Nothing required — arrive at Mizuno by 18:00 to queue before they open.

> 🌸 **Cherry blossoms**: Osaka Castle Park has **late-blooming yaezakura** near Tamatsukuri Gate (southwest). These double cherry blossoms bloom mid-to-late April — check this area specifically.

**Tips**
- Tenma is 1 stop from Osaka Station on JR Loop Line — best budget bar area in all of Kansai
- Bar Freedom: only 10 seats, opens 19:30, no reservations — go early or late
- Bible Club: semi-hidden basement off Dotonbori, speakeasy vibes
- Pace yourself on drinks — you have 7 more days!
- Convenience stores sell strong chuhai (¥150) if you want a cheap pre-game

> 💰 **Estimated Day Cost**: ¥14,000–22,000 (transport ¥700 + attractions ¥1,200 + food ¥4,250–6,400 + nightlife ¥8,000–14,000)
