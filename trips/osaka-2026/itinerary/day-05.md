## Notes

- **Book ahead**: Nothing required — arrive at Mizuno by 18:30 to queue before they open. Bar Nayuta is walk-in but often fills — earlier is better.
- **Cherry blossoms**: Osaka Castle Park has **late-blooming yaezakura** near Tamatsukuri Gate — double cherry blossoms bloom mid-to-late April.
- **Tips**:
  - Bar Nayuta: hidden door near Triangle Park in Amemura; #100 on Asia's 50 Best Bars 2025; no menu, just tell the bartender what you like
  - Ura Namba works best with one big rule: keep moving, 1–2 drinks per place across 3–4 spots — that's the kuidaore way
  - Tenma tachinomi is the best budget alternative if anyone prefers standing bars — 1 stop from Osaka Station on JR Loop Line
  - Convenience stores sell strong chuhai (¥150) if you want a cheap pre-game
  - Pace yourself — Nara + Lamp Bar is tomorrow, another late one
- **Estimated Day Cost**: ¥13,000–21,000 (transport ¥700 + attractions ¥1,200 + food ¥4,250–6,400 + nightlife ¥5,500–9,000 + hotel)
