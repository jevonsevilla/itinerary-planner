## Notes

- **Book ahead**: Nothing required. If substituting Lamp Bar (Nara) for farewell drinks: **reserve ahead — essential** (email or via website).
- **Shimada Shoten open Wednesday**: If doing Osaka nightlife instead of Nara Lamp Bar, Shimada Shoten's underground sake cellar (enter through shop, climb down a ladder, 250+ rare sakes) is open Wednesday and is a unique experience.
- **Cherry blossoms**: Nara has late-blooming "Naranoyaezakura" (double cherry variety) that blooms late April — you may spot some in the park.
- **Tips**:
  - Aquarium takes 2–2.5 hrs to do properly — don't rush through it
  - Deer can be aggressive when they see crackers — bow to them first (they bow back!)
  - Keep bags zipped; deer eat paper and maps
  - **Tonight is the last full-group night** — tomorrow the Cathay Pacific crew splits off after Kobe lunch
  - Bar Nayuta: hidden door near Triangle Park in Amemura — finding it together is a great group memory
- **Estimated Day Cost**: ¥16,000–26,000 (transport ¥1,600 + aquarium ¥2,700 + Nara attractions ¥1,300–1,700 + food ¥5,700–9,000 + farewell drinks ¥3,000–5,000)
