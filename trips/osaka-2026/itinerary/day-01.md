## Notes

- **Book ahead**: Nothing required — buy the Rapi:t ticket at KIX on arrival
- **Cherry blossoms**: Peak was early April; late-blooming yaezakura at Osaka Castle Park bloom mid-to-late April — save for tomorrow
- **Tips**:
  - Buy an IC card (ICOCA or equivalent) at KIX — you'll use it every day
  - Dotonbori is most atmospheric at night with all the neon lights
  - Don't over-schedule tonight — jet lag is real
  - Hozenji Yokocho is a magical 80m lantern-lit alley just steps from Dotonbori — perfect intro to Osaka nightlife
  - Visit the moss-covered Fudo Myo-o statue at Hozenji Temple while there
  - Last trains run ~midnight; hotel is walking distance from Dotonbori
- **Estimated Day Cost**: ¥5,500–8,000 (transport ¥1,520 + food ¥2,500–4,000 + drinks ¥1,500–2,500)
