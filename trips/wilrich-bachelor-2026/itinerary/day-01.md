# Day 1 — Arrival & First Taste of Da Nang
**Date:** 2026-11-19 (Thursday)
**Vibe:** Land, check in, decompress at the villa, then ease into the city with a welcome dinner and a warm-up night out

---

## Morning / Early Afternoon
**[Flexible — varies by flight]** — Arrival at Da Nang International Airport (DAD)

Da Nang airport is small and efficient — immigration and baggage for 9 people typically takes 30–50 minutes. No visa on arrival needed for most ASEAN passport holders; confirm your nationality's requirements before travel.

**Transport to resort:** Pre-arrange a 16-seat private minivan through your hotel concierge or a local tour company (~$25–35 one-way for the group). Much easier than coordinating 2–3 Grab cars with luggage. Drop all bags at the villa, do a quick regroup.

**Recommended accommodation:** Hyatt Regency Danang Resort & Spa (Non Nuoc Beach) or Premier Village Danang Resort (My Khe Beach). Either way, aim for 2 adjacent villas with private pools. Check-in is typically 2–3 PM; ask for early check-in if arriving before noon.

## Afternoon
**2:00 PM – 5:00 PM** — Villa settling & pool time

This is the chill window before the first night out. Drop bags, claim beds, take stock of the villa. Jump in the private pool, have the first cold Saigon Beer or Tiger of the trip. If anyone's hungry, order room service or walk to the resort's beach restaurant for a light lunch.

If arriving early enough, the beach is right there — November swells mean My Khe is better for wading than swimming, but it's still worth a walk down. Water temp is ~25°C.

## Evening
**6:30 PM** — Welcome Dinner at My Hanh Seafood

A MICHELIN Guide Vietnam 2025 honoree and one of Da Nang's most acclaimed restaurants. Oceanfront terrace, live fish tanks, exceptional fresh shellfish — crabs, prawns, scallops, grilled fish. This is the first proper group meal of the trip; make it count.

**Booking:** Call or email at least 1–2 weeks ahead to reserve a table for 9. Specify it's a bachelor party group and ask for the terrace. Typical spend: **~$50–70 per person with drinks**.

After dinner, head back to the villas for pre-drinks — pool area pre-drinks are the move. Set the tone for the week, crack open duty-free bottles, and let people get properly acquainted (or reacquainted) before heading out.

## Night
**10:00 PM** — Warm-Up Night: OQ Lounge Pub (Bach Dang Riverfront)

**Address:** 18–20 Bach Dang Street, Hai Chau District (Han River waterfront)
**Hours:** 8 PM – 3 AM

OQ Lounge is the best-value large club in Da Nang and a perfect warm-up for the week — 400-person capacity, riverside location, house and EDM sets, and genuinely good Happy Hour pricing (8–10 PM: drinks from 100,000 VND / ~$4). By the time the group arrives at 10 PM, the place is hitting its stride.

Book a **Premium Sofa** (up to 8 guests, ~2,650,000 VND / ~$101 minimum — not applicable Fridays, so Thursday is ideal). For 9 people, book 2 adjacent sofas or one standard sofa + a standing table. Happy Hour runs until 10 PM, so arriving closer to 9:30 PM captures the tail end of discounted drinks.

Thursday night is not the peak party night of the trip, so this is intentionally low-key — a way to get everyone into the same room, feel the Da Nang vibe, and stay out until 1–2 AM without burning anyone out before the big nights.

**~1:30–2:00 AM** — Late-night snack at An Thuong Night Market

An Thuong Night Market runs until 11 PM most nights but the area stays lively. Grab bánh mì, grilled corn, and pho from the surrounding streets. Good way to wrap the first night.

## Logistics
- Pre-book a 16-seat private minivan for airport pickup — coordinate with hotel concierge or a local tour operator before departure
- OQ Lounge is on Bach Dang Street, Han River waterfront — approximately 15–20 min Grab from the resort area (~$4–7). Book 2 GrabCars for 9 people
- After clubs: 2 GrabCars back to the resort. Have the "point person" coordinate in the club lobby — don't try to split 9 people into cars outside on the street

## Notes & Tips
- Designate a "point person" for the trip (the one who stays the most sober each night to manage Grab bookings and headcounts). Rotate nightly if needed.
- Download Grab before arrival — it's the primary transport app. Have VND cash or a card loaded.
- Day 1 is intentionally paced — big nights are Day 2 and Day 3. Encourage everyone to stay out until midnight–1 AM rather than pushing it.
- If the group has jet lag (especially from long-haul), the pool afternoon and a 6:30 PM dinner gives time to reset.
- Confirm villa check-in times with the hotel and arrange luggage storage if arriving early.
