# Day 2 — Beach Club Day & The Big Friday Night
**Date:** 2026-11-20 (Friday)
**Vibe:** Recharge at the beach club, afternoon surf, then hit ADM Club for the peak bachelor party night

---

## Morning
**10:00 AM** — Slow villa breakfast

No alarms. No schedules before 10. Everyone gathers at the villa/pool for breakfast — order room service or hit the resort's beach restaurant. Full spreads, fresh fruit, strong Vietnamese coffee (cà phê đá). Let Day 1 shake off naturally.

**Vietnamese iced coffee (cà phê sữa đá)** is non-negotiable — thick, dark robusta drip coffee over sweetened condensed milk and ice. Order it everywhere.

## Afternoon
**11:30 AM – 2:00 PM** — Esco Beach Club (Vo Nguyen Giap Street, An Hai Ward)

Da Nang's best beach club for a group setup. Oceanfront, infinity pool, daybeds and cabanas, live DJs from early afternoon, magicians and fire performers in the evening, and a full bar service. This is the pre-game zone for the biggest night of the trip.

**Booking:** Call ahead (0236 3955 668) to reserve a cabana or group daybeds for 9. Beach clubs in Da Nang typically have a spend minimum rather than a flat entry fee. For 9 people ordering cocktails and food through the afternoon, you'll comfortably exceed most minimums.

The vibe here from noon is relaxed and social — pool cocktails, seafood snacks, music building through the afternoon. Perfect Friday warm-up energy.

**2:30 PM – 4:30 PM** — Surf Lessons at My Khe Beach

My Khe has beginner-to-intermediate surf conditions. November swells can actually give it a bit more punch than peak dry season — good fun for a group. **Da Nang Outdoor Adventures** (danangadventures.com) is the top operator; they can handle groups of 9 with advance booking.

**Cost:** ~$30–45 per person (lesson + board rental, approximately 2 hours).

Some guys surf, some stay at Esco Beach — split freely. Regroup at the resort by 5:30 PM.

## Evening
**6:00 PM – 8:30 PM** — Resort pre-drinks & dinner prep

Back at the villas. Pool pre-drinks, get changed, eat something solid before the big night. The resort has a beach restaurant, or order room service — keep it light: rice dishes, spring rolls, something to line the stomach. This is not the night for a long sit-down dinner; the focus is ADM Club.

**8:30 PM** — Optional: Sophie Mixology (150 Bach Dang Street, Hai Chau Ward)
Hours: 10 PM – 2 AM, but the bar area is open earlier.

If the group wants a quick pre-club stop near ADM, Sophie Mixology on Bach Dang is a good shout — Han River views, EDM and house music, local crowd. One round of drinks, 45 minutes, then move.

## Night
**10:00 PM** — ADM Club (Peak Bachelor Night)

**Address:** 2/9 Street, Hoa Cuong Bac Ward, Hai Chau District
**Hours:** 7:30 PM – 3 AM
**Hotline:** 0982 818 199

This is the main event of the trip. ADM is the most-hyped club in Da Nang — state-of-the-art sound and light system, international DJ bookings, and genuinely one of the best club experiences in Vietnam. Friday night here is peak.

**VIP Table recommendation for 9 people:**
- **VVIP Sofa (1–15 guests):** 10-bottle minimum — ~35,990,000 VND (~$1,400 USD + 10% VAT + 5% service charge = ~$1,617 total / ~$180 per person)
- Alternative: **Ultra Sofa VVIP (1–20 guests):** 2 bottles R ACE spirit — ~41,880,000 VND (~$1,630 + taxes = ~$1,882 total / ~$209 per person)

The VVIP Sofa (10-bottle package) is the better value for 9 guys — that's 10 bottles of spirits split 9 ways, which is the correct amount for a proper bachelor party night.

**Book this at least 2–3 weeks before arrival.** WhatsApp or call the hotline. Confirm the booking a few days before. This is the one booking that cannot be left to chance on a Friday in November.

**What to expect:** The club builds from about 10 PM with a DJ set that escalates into full peak-hour production around midnight. International and regional DJs regularly perform. Table service with bottle packages means no queuing at the bar — dedicated table host, mixers, and ice service. Expect to stay until 2:30–3 AM.

**~3:00 AM** — After-party options:
1. **Koto Club** (254 Vo Nguyen Giap Street) — 24/7 license; beach-adjacent; good for whoever wants to keep going. EDM and themed dance performances.
2. **An Thuong Night Market area** — Street food to soak up the night. Bánh mì and grilled corn for the walk home.
3. Back to the villas — the private pool is calling.

## Logistics
- Grab from resort to Esco Beach: ~10–15 min, $4–6 (2 GrabCars)
- Surf lesson location: My Khe Beach — most operators pick up from the beach area near your resort
- Grab from resort to ADM Club: approximately 15–20 min, $5–8 (2 GrabCars). Pre-book the return trip through the app before leaving the club — Friday nights can be busy.
- ADM Club address is in Hai Chau District (inland, not beachside) — don't confuse with beach club areas. Input the address directly into Grab.

## Notes & Tips
- **ADM booking is the most critical logistical task for the whole trip.** Do this 2–4 weeks before Nov 19. Contact: 0982 818 199 (WhatsApp or call). Request a VVIP sofa for "a group of 9 for a bachelor party."
- Dress code at ADM: Smart casual minimum. No flip-flops, no singlets. Da Nang clubs are generally lenient but ADM enforces standards. Have everyone in a collared shirt or equivalent.
- Surf lesson: Book in advance with Da Nang Outdoor Adventures. Mention it's a group of 9. They can split into ability levels.
- Esco Beach cabana: Call (0236 3955 668) a week ahead for the group booking. Minimum spend requirements are easier to meet with a group this size.
- Hydration: It's a big night. Drink water at the table — good table hosts will bring it. Eat before leaving the resort.
