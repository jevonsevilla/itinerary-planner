# Day 3 — Ba Na Hills & Peak Bachelor Night (New Oriental)
**Date:** 2026-11-21 (Saturday)
**Vibe:** Late start, iconic Vietnamese landmark, then the biggest Saturday night of the trip at Da Nang's most legendary club

---

## Morning
**[No alarm]** — Full recovery morning after ADM

Nobody is awake before 10 AM after a Friday night at ADM. Plan accordingly. Room service breakfast at the villa, coffee by the pool, sunscreen. The Ba Na Hills departure is set for late morning to accommodate this — if booking a group tour, specify a 10:30–11 AM pickup rather than a 7–8 AM departure.

**11:00 AM** — Ba Na Hills departure (private group transfer)

Ba Na Hills is a full-day experience at 1,400m altitude — French colonial hill station, the world-famous Golden Bridge (giant stone hands holding a cable bridge), Fantasy Park indoor theme park, craft beer stops, and panoramic mountain views. Iconic, genuinely impressive, and perfect for Day 3 when the group needs an outdoor adventure with no major physical effort required.

**Transport:** Book a **private 16-seat minivan** for the group round-trip. Hotel concierge can arrange this, or book through a local tour operator via Klook/GetYourGuide. ~$80–120 for the van (shared equally = ~$9–13 per person). The drive is approximately 30 km from the city center, ~45 min.

**Group tour vs. private transfer:** The most seamless option is a private transfer (van with driver) that drops you at the Ba Na Hills cable car entrance and picks you up at a set time (say, 5 PM). This lets the group move at their own pace without a tour group schedule.

## Afternoon
**11:45 AM – 4:30 PM** — Ba Na Hills

**Entry:** ~900,000 VND (~$35) per adult, includes cable car (the world's longest single-cable car system — 5,801 meters). Buy tickets online in advance to skip the queue: banahillstickets.com or through Klook.

**Itinerary within Ba Na Hills:**
- **11:45 AM:** Cable car ascent (15–20 min ride with stunning views)
- **12:30 PM:** Golden Bridge — the photo of the trip. Stone hands rising from the mist holding a golden bridge; best lighting in early afternoon. Allow 20–30 min here; it will be busy on a Saturday.
- **1:15 PM:** Lunch at one of the hill station restaurants (buffet available, or à la carte at French Village restaurants). Budget ~$15–25 per person.
- **2:30 PM:** Fantasy Park indoor amusement — 4D cinema, games, arcade, bumper cars. Optional and silly in the best possible way for a group of 9.
- **3:30 PM:** Beer stops at the French Village craft beer bars and wine cellars. Da Nang Brewing Co. has a presence here. Order a round at altitude.
- **4:30 PM:** Cable car descent, back to the van.

**5:15 PM** — Back at the resort. Pool time, showers, rest.

## Evening
**7:00 PM** — Dinner at Olivia's Prime Steakhouse

After yesterday's seafood blowout and a big day on the mountain, a proper steak dinner for 9 is the right call before New Oriental. Imported cuts, premium wines, a private-room setup if requested. Heavy food before a big club night = strategic.

**Booking:** Reserve for 9 at least a week in advance. Budget ~$60–80 per person with wine/cocktails.

**9:00 PM** — Villa pre-drinks

Head back to the villa before the club. An hour of pre-drinks by the pool — local gin/tonic, Red Bull mixes, whatever's left from the duty-free. Get changed, coordinate Grab pickups. Tonight: New Oriental.

## Night
**10:00 PM** — New Oriental Club (New Phuong Dong)

**Address:** 20 Dong Da Street, Hai Chau Ward
**Hours:** 8 PM – 4 AM (Friday/Saturday)
**Hotline:** Contact via their social media or the number on vietnamnightlife.com

New Oriental is the oldest and most famous club in Da Nang — over 30 years of history, fully revamped in 2025. Three-tier stage with LED screens, serious sound system, massive dance floor. Saturday here draws the biggest crowd of the week.

**VIP Table recommendation for 9 people:**
- **Ultra VVIP (1–20 guests):** 6 bottles — ~20,000,000 VND (~$760 + 10% VAT + 6% service charge = ~$895 total / ~$99 per person). This is exceptional value for 9 people — 6 bottles of spirits for under $100 per person including table.
- **VVIP Sofa (1–15 guests):** 3 bottles — ~10,000,000 VND (~$380 + taxes = ~$449 / ~$50 per person). More modest but still comfortable.

For a bachelor party on Saturday night, the Ultra VVIP at ~$99/person is the call. Six bottles, dedicated host, best positioning on the floor.

**Book this in advance** — New Oriental on a Saturday fills VIP sections fast. Contact 1–2 weeks before arrival.

Saturday at New Oriental runs until 4 AM. The group can easily stay until 2–2:30 AM (or later for those still standing).

**~2:30–3:00 AM** — Options:
1. **Koto Club** (254 Vo Nguyen Giap, open 24/7) — the after-party venue. Located near the beach, different vibe, themed dance nights. For whoever wants to keep moving.
2. **An Thuong Night Market streets** — late-night bánh mì, grilled corn, cold beer from a sidewalk stall. The perfect punctuation to a big night.

## Logistics
- Ba Na Hills is ~30 km from Da Nang city center (45 min by road). Pre-book a private 16-seat minivan — hotel concierge can arrange, or use Klook/GetYourGuide for a private group tour with driver.
- Ba Na Hills tickets: Buy online in advance at banahillstickets.com to avoid Saturday queues. The cable car queue on weekends can add 30–45 min without pre-purchased tickets.
- New Oriental is on Dong Da Street, Hai Chau District — about 15 min Grab from most resort areas ($5–7). 2 GrabCars needed for 9 people.
- Saturday night Grab demand is high after 2 AM — have the point person book return cars from inside the club, not at the curb.

## Notes & Tips
- Ba Na Hills is genuinely impressive but can be crowded on weekends. Moving as a group of 9, get the Golden Bridge photos done early before afternoon tour groups pile in.
- The altitude at Ba Na Hills (1,400m) means it can be cooler and windier than sea level — bring a light layer. November wind chill at altitude is real.
- Buy Ba Na Hills tickets online as a group to save time. Group discount may be available through tour operators.
- New Oriental Saturday night is the second peak club experience of the trip (after ADM Friday). Two premium club nights back-to-back is the correct move — gives the group the full range of Da Nang's club scene.
- Day 4 is a recovery/chill day. Everyone can sleep late on Sunday.
